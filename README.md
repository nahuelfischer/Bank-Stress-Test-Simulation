# Bank Stress Test Simulation
This tool simulates risks a bank faces. Especially in harsh market conditions, major losses can occur which the banks somehow must manage. As there is uncertainty about future returns, stress tests can very well simulate the losses a bank faces in such bad conditions and lead to responsible risk taking inside the bank.
